Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Last Updated

This Privacy Policy was last updated on April 2026.

Who We Are

Qatar Live Weather (qatarliveweather.com) is an independent weather information service providing real-time weather data for Qatar and its municipalities. The site is operated by mohammedlglg.

Data We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information directly. However, the following third-party services may collect data when you use this site:

  • Google Analytics 4 — collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, device type, and general geographic region. This helps us understand how visitors use the site. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdSense — may serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history using cookies. You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings →
  • Geolocation (optional) — if you click "Use my location," your browser requests your GPS coordinates to find the nearest weather station. This data is processed locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

This site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — help us understand how visitors interact with the site. These are only set if you accept cookies.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense) — used to deliver relevant advertisements. These are only set if you accept cookies.
  • Functional cookies — we store your cookie consent preference in localStorage so we don't ask repeatedly.

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Data Sources

Weather data displayed on this site is sourced from the following third-party services:

  • wttr.in — provides current conditions, forecasts, and astronomy data.
  • Aladhan API — provides Islamic prayer times based on geographic coordinates.
  • Wikipedia — provides informational summaries about locations.

Disclaimer: Weather data on this site is provided for general informational purposes only and is not official data from the Qatar Meteorology Department (QMD). For official forecasts, visit met.gov.qa →

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR or other privacy laws, including the right to:

  • Access data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Withdraw your cookie consent at any time

To exercise these rights or if you have questions, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
