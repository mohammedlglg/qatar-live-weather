About Qatar Live Weather

A free, bilingual, interactive weather map covering every municipality in Qatar — built for residents, expats, and visitors.

What Is This?

Qatar Live Weather is a real-time interactive weather dashboard that shows current conditions across all Qatar municipalities. It pulls live data from wttr.in and presents it as colour-coded map markers you can click for a full forecast breakdown.

Each location shows: current temperature and feels-like, 3-day forecast, hourly forecast strip, UV index, humidity, wind speed and direction, visibility, precipitation probability, prayer times, sun & moon astronomy, and more.

Why We Built It

Qatar's climate is unique — extreme summer heat, significant dust and sandstorm events, and high UV levels make weather information genuinely important for daily life. Most global weather apps don't cover Qatar's smaller municipalities, and many don't offer Arabic language support.

Qatar Live Weather fills that gap: a fast, free, bilingual tool designed specifically for people living in or visiting Qatar.

Features

  • ✅ Real-time temperature map — colour-coded markers across 7 municipalities
  • ✅ 3-day weather forecast with daily details
  • ✅ Hourly forecast strip with rain probability
  • ✅ UV index with safety guidance
  • ✅ Heat stress index — is it safe to be outside?
  • ✅ Dust and sandstorm alerts
  • ✅ Prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
  • ✅ Sun & moon astronomy (sunrise, sunset, moon phase)
  • ✅ Wind compass and direction
  • ✅ Bilingual: full English and Arabic (RTL) support
  • ✅ Dark mode
  • ✅ PWA — installable as a mobile app
  • ✅ Share any location via URL or native share

Regions Covered

The map covers all seven Qatari municipalities (Baladiyat):

  • 🏙️ Doha (Ad Dawhah) — 14 locations including West Bay, Lusail, The Pearl, Qatar University, and more
  • 🏘️ Ar Rayyan — Education City, Al Waab, Abu Samra
  • 🌊 Al Wakrah — Mesaieed, Sealine Beach, Inland Sea
  • 🏭 Al Khor & Dhekra — Ras Laffan Industrial City
  • 🏝️ Ash Shamal — Al Zubara, Al Ruwais
  • 🐪 Ash Sheehaniya — Dukhan, Camel Race Track
  • 🌿 Ad Daayen — Umm Slal Mohammed, Al Kheesa

Data Sources

All weather data comes from freely available, reputable sources:

  • wttr.in — weather conditions, forecasts, astronomy (powered by WorldWeatherOnline)
  • Aladhan API — prayer times using the Umm Al-Qura calculation method
  • Wikipedia — location summaries via the Wikipedia REST API

Note: This is an independent service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Qatar Meteorology Department. Data is for informational purposes only.
